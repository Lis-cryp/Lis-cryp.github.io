Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how Dreamer Studio collects, uses, stores, and deletes information when users access creator tools, connect Pinterest accounts, and use related publishing features.

1. Information we collect

  • Account details such as email address, display name, and service preferences.
  • Pinterest account identifiers and permissions granted through secure OAuth integration.
  • Draft content metadata, publishing preferences, and connection status.
  • Technical logs used for security, error monitoring, and service improvement.

2. How we use information

  • To authenticate users and maintain creator dashboards.
  • To connect Pinterest accounts and perform user-approved publishing actions.
  • To display account connection state and connection history.
  • To provide customer support, security monitoring, and legal compliance.

3. OAuth tokens and storage

Access tokens and refresh tokens obtained through Pinterest OAuth are stored only for the connected account, using secure server-side storage and access controls. Tokens are never sold, never shared with unrelated third parties, and are used solely to provide the requested service to the user who authorized the connection.

4. Cookies and analytics

Dreamer Studio may use essential cookies for authentication and session management. Basic analytics may be used to understand page performance, security, and product usage patterns. Analytics are not used to create hidden behavioral profiles or sell Pinterest-derived information.

5. API permissions

Dreamer Studio requests only the Pinterest permissions required for account connection, profile access, and user-approved publishing features. Requested scopes are reviewed regularly and reduced when no longer required.

6. Data sharing

We do not sell personal information or Pinterest-derived information. We may share limited information with infrastructure providers that help us host, secure, and operate the service, subject to confidentiality and data protection obligations.

7. Data retention and deletion

We retain personal data and connected-account data only as long as necessary to provide the service, meet legal obligations, resolve disputes, or maintain security records. Users may request account deletion, token revocation, and removal of associated data through the contact methods listed on the Contact page.

8. User rights

  • Request access to stored personal data.
  • Request correction of inaccurate account information.
  • Request deletion of account data and connected Pinterest tokens.
  • Disconnect Pinterest access at any time.

9. Contact

For privacy requests, user data deletion, or Pinterest-related compliance questions, use the support details on the Contact page.
